Popular Behavior Tips

Barking Issues

Solutions for excessive barking

  • Provide adequate exercise and mental stimulation
  • Teach the "quiet" command with positive reinforcement
  • Avoid punishment as it can increase anxiety
  • Identify and address the root cause of barking
Watch Training Video

Separation Anxiety

Helping pets cope with being alone

  • Gradually increase alone time starting with short periods
  • Leave comforting items with your scent
  • Use calming sounds or pheromone diffusers
  • Create a safe, comfortable space for your pet
Watch Training Video

Litter Box Problems

Addressing common litter box issues

  • Keep the litter box extremely clean
  • Try different types of litter and boxes
  • Ensure the box is in a quiet, accessible location
  • Rule out medical issues with a vet visit
Watch Training Video

Leash Pulling

Teaching your dog to walk politely on leash

  • Stop moving when your dog pulls
  • Reward your dog for staying by your side
  • Use front-clip harnesses for better control
  • Be consistent with your training approach
Watch Training Video

Scratching Furniture

Redirecting scratching behavior

  • Provide appropriate scratching posts and pads
  • Use catnip to attract your cat to scratching posts
  • Apply double-sided tape to furniture edges
  • Trim your cat's nails regularly
Watch Training Video

Socialization Issues

Helping your dog interact positively with others

  • Expose your dog gradually to new experiences
  • Use positive reinforcement for calm behavior
  • Respect your dog's comfort zone
  • Consider professional help for serious fear or aggression
