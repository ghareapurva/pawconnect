What to Do If You've Lost or Found a Pet

If You've Lost a Pet

  • Search your neighborhood immediately
  • Notify local animal shelters and veterinarians
  • Create flyers with a clear photo and contact info
  • Post on local social media groups and community boards
  • Leave out familiar items with your scent

If You've Found a Pet

  • Check for identification tags or microchip
  • Take to a vet to scan for microchip
  • Post found notices in the area
  • Notify local animal shelters
  • Approach carefully - scared animals may behave unpredictably

Prevention Tips

  • Ensure your pet always wears a collar with ID tags
  • Microchip your pet and keep registration updated
  • Keep recent photos of your pet
  • Secure your yard with proper fencing
  • Leash your pet during walks
